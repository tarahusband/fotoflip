Privacy Policy

Last updated June 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how FotoFlip collects, uses, and stores your information when you use the app.

Information We Collect

FotoFlip collects the following categories of personal information:

  • Name and email address (from your Google account)
  • Phone number (from the access request form)
  • Seller handle and business name
  • Google profile photo
  • Photos you upload
  • Inventory and listing details you create
  • App activity and error details
  • Communication preferences (email and SMS consent)
  • IP address and browser information (recorded at consent)

How We Use It

  • Creating and managing your account
  • Providing access to FotoFlip
  • Generating listing details using AI
  • Managing your inventory
  • Troubleshooting support issues
  • Sending account and access updates
  • Improving the product

Third-Party Services

We share data with the following services to deliver FotoFlip:

  • Google — account sign-in and authentication
  • Cloudinary — photo storage and delivery
  • Anthropic (Claude) — AI-generated listing descriptions
  • Railway — app hosting and infrastructure

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you request deletion, we will remove your account and associated data within 30 days.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect and why
  • Request a copy of your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of sale or sharing of personal information (FotoFlip does not currently sell personal information)

To exercise these rights, submit a request at Privacy Request Form or email john@husband.llc.
